**Kindi** is an advanced AI-powered voice assistant designed to streamline productivity through intelligent automation. Recognized with the **Coup de Cœur** award and placing **4th** at the AI Crafters Hackathon (02/2025), Kindi leverages a multi-agent architecture to handle complex workflows via natural language.

### Key Features

*   **Task Setter Agent**: Effortlessly schedule tasks in **Google Calendar** using simple voice commands.
*   **Project Planning Agent**: Generate full project timelines and deadlines automatically using LLMs, directly synced to your calendar.
*   **Meeting Agent**: Automate your meeting documentation. Kindi transcribes recordings with **Whisper**, stores notes in **Google Drive**, and links them directly to your calendar events.

Developed with a focus on seamless integration and voice-first interaction, Kindi transforms how you manage your time and projects.
